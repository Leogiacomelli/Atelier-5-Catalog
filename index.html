ATELIER 5

by community of Artists

Atelier five is a unique brand of women clothing. It is classic with a twist as we work with a community of Fine Art Artists to create a unique line of beautiful prints.
We are happy to give a platform to support artists from around the world.
The style of our dresses and tops is classic and sophisticated. Lots of fine details are incorporated in the garment, different fabrics are used and combined to create a piece that will gracefully adorn a woman â€˜s body. We are proud to say that all our garments are made in our workshop based in Philadelphia. A team of six sewing ladies are crafting each and everyone of them.

OUR ARTISTS

We are constantly on the look out for new artists. If you know anyone interested to use our line to show their art and advertise themselves, we are happy to consider them. Our artists are coming from literally around the world and they bring with them their own sense of colors, and esthetics. We invite you to take the time to look at their websites for your own pleasure.
